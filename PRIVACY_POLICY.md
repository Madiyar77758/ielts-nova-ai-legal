# Privacy Policy for IELTS Nova AI

**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how IELTS Nova AI ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

By using IELTS Nova AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address (via Google Sign-In or direct registration)
- Display name
- Profile photo (optional, if provided via Google account)
- A unique user identifier generated by our authentication system

### 1.2 Profile Information
During onboarding, we collect:
- Your name
- Reasons for studying IELTS
- Whether you have taken IELTS before
- Your current English proficiency level
- Your target IELTS band score
- Sections you find most difficult
- Daily practice time commitment

This information is used to personalize your learning experience.

### 1.3 Learning Activity
As you use the App, we collect:
- Test attempts and their timestamps
- Answers to practice questions
- Mock exam scores (Reading, Listening, Writing, Speaking)
- Speaking audio recordings (temporarily, for AI evaluation only)
- Writing essay submissions (for AI evaluation)
- Daily streak data
- Time spent in different sections

### 1.4 Device and Technical Information
We automatically collect:
- Device model and operating system version
- App version
- Crash reports and performance data
- Approximate location (country level only, derived from IP address)

### 1.5 Microphone Access
The App requests permission to access your device's microphone solely for the Speaking practice section. Audio recordings are sent to our AI evaluation service (Google Gemini) for grading and are not stored permanently on our servers after evaluation is complete.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide personalized IELTS preparation content
- Evaluate your Writing and Speaking responses using AI
- Track your learning progress and provide feedback
- Calculate and display your scores
- Send you practice reminders (only if enabled in settings)
- Improve our AI evaluation algorithms
- Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

IELTS Nova AI uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

### 3.1 Google Firebase
We use Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions) to authenticate users, store your progress data, and host audio content. Firebase is operated by Google LLC.
Privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### 3.2 Google Gemini AI
Your Writing essays and Speaking audio recordings are sent to Google's Gemini AI service for automated evaluation and feedback. Data sent to Gemini is processed under Google's terms.
Privacy policy: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms

### 3.3 Google Sign-In
If you sign in using a Google account, we receive your email, name, and profile picture from Google.
Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which are protected by industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Encryption at rest
- Access controls and authentication

While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and learning progress for as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account:
- Your profile information is deleted within 30 days
- Your learning history is anonymized or deleted
- Speaking audio recordings, if any remain in temporary processing, are deleted within 7 days

You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us at the email below.
