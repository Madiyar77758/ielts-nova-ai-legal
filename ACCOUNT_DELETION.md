# Account Deletion Request — IELTS Nova AI

**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

If you wish to delete your IELTS Nova AI account and all associated data, you have two options:

---

## Option 1: Delete From Within the App

1. Open the IELTS Nova AI app on your device
2. Sign in to your account
3. Go to **Settings** (bottom right tab)
4. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Log Out**
5. To request full account deletion, contact us via Option 2 below

> **Note:** A built-in "Delete Account" button is being implemented and will be available in a future update. Until then, please use Option 2 to request deletion.

---

## Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

To delete your IELTS Nova AI account, send an email to:

**📧 ryancole.biz17@gmail.com**

### What to include in your email:

- **Subject line:** "Account Deletion Request"
- **Email body:**
  - Your registered email address (the one you used to sign in with Google)
  - Your full name (as registered in the app)
  - A brief statement: "I request the deletion of my IELTS Nova AI account and all associated data."

### What happens next:

1. We will verify your identity within **3 business days**
2. Once verified, we will delete your account
3. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete
4. **Total processing time:** within 30 days from your request

---

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be **permanently removed** from our servers:

- ✅ Your email address and account credentials
- ✅ Your display name and profile photo
- ✅ Your onboarding profile information (target score, goals, level, etc.)
- ✅ All your test attempts and scores (Reading, Listening, Writing, Speaking)
- ✅ All Writing essays you submitted
- ✅ All Speaking audio recordings (if any remain in temporary processing)
- ✅ Daily streak data and learning analytics
- ✅ Settings preferences

---

## What Data May Be Retained

For legal, security, and operational reasons, some data may be retained for a limited period:

- 📋 **Anonymized analytics:** Aggregate usage statistics with no personal identifiers (retained indefinitely for service improvement)
- 📋 **Legal records:** If we are legally required to retain certain records (e.g., due to fraud investigation), they will be kept only as long as legally required
- 📋 **Backups:** Account data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being permanently purged

After 90 days from your deletion request, **no personally identifiable information** about you will remain in our systems.

---

## Important Notes

- **Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.** Once deleted, you cannot recover your progress, scores, or settings.
- If you create a new account using the same email later, it will be a **fresh account** with no connection to the deleted one.
- If you are subscribed to email notifications (in the future), you will be unsubscribed automatically.
